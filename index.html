Post-Study

Our study was based around The Usability Testing Research Framework which consisted of closed and open ended questions in a pre and post task assessment. Four quality components were targeted such as Learnability (how quickly users were able to perform basic tasks when using the system for first time), Efficiency + Memorability (how fast users can perform tasks based on their previous experience and interactions), and Errors (errors that user might make such as navigating to a wrong page to check the grades and then correcting themself).
